    • Course Description:-

    The aim of this module is to provide a strong set of principles and techniques for structuring and representing requirements and architectures.

    Aims-

  • The aim of this module is to provide a strong set of principles and techniques for structuring and representing requirements and architectures.

    • Learning Outcomes:-

  • Describe the role of requirements engineering and architecture design and assessment for the development of critical systems
  • Explain different types of requirements
  • Perform quality review of requirements
  • Describe and participate in techniques for requirements elicitation, representation validation, re-use and traceability
  • Select and apply architectural strategies to address requirements
  • Assess the selection and application of architectural strategies
  • Apply appropriate notations for architecture representation
  • Discuss the state of the art and future directions in architecture modelling

    Contents:-

    Topics Covered-
  • Introduction and requirements principles (Introduction; Principles of good requirements engineering)
  • Requirements techniques (Requirements Elicitation and Modelling; Use of Natural Language; Requirements reuse;)
  • Requirements management and Introduction to Architecture (Requirements validation; Requirements management; Introduction to Architecture based development)
  • Architectural techniques (Principles of architecture design and descriptions; Representation of architectures; Architectures for reuse)
  • Architectural assessment (Architecture evaluation; Architectures and safety; Mapping architectures to requirements and implementation)
